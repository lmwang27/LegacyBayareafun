Palo Alto Volleyball Club

We play volleyball every Sunday morning from 10am to 12am at Robles Park. There is no membership fee. Contribution is at cost for repairs/replacement. All welcome to join, participate in the free and fun exercise, and great banter, more the merrier. The club is open to all level. If you are interested, please email your phone number to funsbayarea@gmail.com to add you to our what's up group to get latest information. Our page in next door.
