Place: SAP Center | 525 W Santa Clara, San Jose, CA 95113
Time: 11月11日周日，上午10点到12点
Fee: 免费
Fee: 免费
Link: http://www.uvcscc.org/VetParadeInfo.htm
自1919年以来，圣克拉拉县Santa Clara County 联合退伍军人委员会（UVC）每年都会在老兵节这天，举行老兵日仪式并举行退伍军人节游行。 11月11日，在圣何塞市SAP中心将举行第98届退伍军人节游行，向所有服务过军队的人和正在服役的人致敬。游行从97号高速公路开始，沿圣克拉拉街（Santa Clara Street）一直到市场街（Market Street），终点在圣卡洛斯街(San Carlos Street)。

Princess and the Pea in the Playhouse

Place: Palo Alto Children's Theatre Palo Alto, CA US
Time: Sat 11/17/18 - Sun 11/18/18
Fee: $10.00 - $12.00
Link: https://cityofpaloalto.org/gov/depts/csd/theatre/Default.asp
Palo Alto Children’s Theatre exists to nurture the intellectual, artistic, and personal development of youth through multi-arts education, performing opportunities, and extraordinary theatre experiences. At the Children's Theatre, the process and product are of equal importance in creating excellent theatre for young audiences. Through multi-arts education, the Children’s Theatre develops lifelong skills such as critical and creative thinking, cooperation, and aesthetic sensitivity. A theatre and an educational institution, the Children’s Theatre provides classes and programming designed to provide hands-on learning experiences for youth age 3 through high school. This invaluable teaching tool develops skills and provides an excellent opportunity to develop participants’ theatrical knowledge. Performing arts education opportunities include onsite classes, camps and production experiences, as well as theatrical Outreach Productions (grades 3-5) and Dance In Schools classes (grades K-2) in all twelve PAUSD Elementary Schools. The Palo Alto Children's Theatre is ADA Compliant. Persons with disabilities who require auxiliary aids or services in using City Facilities, services or programs may contact the Disability Act Director, City of Palo Alto, 250 Hamilton Ave., Palo Alto, CA 94301 (650) 329-2550 (voice) or (650) 328-1199 (TDD).
