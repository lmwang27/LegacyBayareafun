The steps to apply for a class C California driver license with the Department of Motor Vehicles (DMV) are outlined below steps. Each step is meant to be read in order. You may be referred to additional publications which can be found on DMV official web site https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/dmv/detail/pubs/brochures/fast_facts/ffdl07 .

Step 1: Before Visiting a DMV Field Office

You need to know the rules and driving knowledge. Besides reading the California Driver Handbook https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/dmv/detail/pubs/hdbk/driver_handbook_toc, you can also watch youtube videos. Just searching “California DMV written test 2018” from youtube, you will get many result such as https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=mR8cgpiFPOk&t=0s&list=WL&index=2 and https://www.youtube.com/results?search_query=california+dmv+written+test+2018

You need to booking appoint for the vision and knowledge test. Some DMVs are really busy which are very hard to book an appointment, some DMV is not that busy. Just try to search some open slot and book the knowledge testing.

Step 2: Vision Test

Vision test will do before knowledge test. Reading glasses are allowed for the vision test. Ensure you bring your corrective lenses if needed.

Step 3: Knowledge Test

You can prepared your knowledge test by reading the California Driver Handbook https://www.dmv.ca.gov/portal/dmv/detail/pubs/hdbk/driver_handbook_toc or watch the youtube videos or you can do both too.

Step 4: Driving Test

The image above shows the driving test score card. You can try to practice according to the score card. DMV also provides two serials video on youtube, which are high quality. Them are worth to watch.
