Bay Area Free Kids and Family Magic Show

Join us every other Saturday at 2:00 p.m. for free magic shows performed by local artists from the Silicon Valley chapter of the International Brotherhood of Magicians (Ring 216). There are two different shows by two different performers. One show starts at 2:00 and the other starts at 2:30.

​Then stick around to learn magic during a free workshop. It’s perfect for kids, adults, and everyone in between. This workshop is free and no reservations are required. Just show up and have fun!

Place: The Art of Magic - San Jose, 460 Lincoln Ave., San Jose, California 95126
Time: Jane 12 2pm, Jan 26 2pm, Feb 9 2pm, Feb 23 2pm, March 9 2pm, March 23 2pm, April 6 2pm, April 20 2pm, May 4 2pm, May 18 2pm,
Fee: Free​
Link: https://www.facebook.com/events/2051306698443319/
